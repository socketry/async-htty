HTTY is thin.

HTTP/2 does the rest.

# Translation

HTTYは薄い。
あとはHTTP/2が引き受ける。

---

First: HTTY itself is thin. The bootstrap is nine bytes, and after that the bytes are raw. Concurrency, framing, request lifecycle, all of that is HTTP/2's job. HTTY does not reinvent any of it.
