# Translation

---

And third: don't reinvent the wheel. Decades of work have gone into making browsers excellent at rich UI — layout, fonts, animations, accessibility, internationalisation, all of it. Use what is already there. HTTY is what lets you put a browser in front of *any* program that knows how to speak HTTP.
